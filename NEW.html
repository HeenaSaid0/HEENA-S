Business Technology Early College High School

Business Technolgy Early College High School is an innovative new school that enables students to earn a high school diploma, an associate’s degree and gain relevant work experience in the business technology field, with the mission of combining academic excellence and technical aptitude.

Forum | Website

Queensborough Community College

A core academic curriculum designed and taught by faculty at Queensborough will help students develop a strong liberal arts and sciences and technological education. BTECH students will take the same courses as Queensborough students in Business and Internet Technology as well as other related programs.

Forum | Website

SAP

The school is in partnership with SAP – an international company that specializes in business enterprise software. Students will enroll in dual-credited courses and participate in work based learning experiences, such as mentoring, job site visits, apprenticeship and internships. Successful completion of the high school and college degree programs, earns the students opportunities for employment at SAP or in the hundreds of companies around the globe who run SAP software.
